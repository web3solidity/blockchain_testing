## Security and Web3

In this repository, we will be building and exploring a variety of **Web3** applications, including but not limited to:

- **Decentralized Finance (DeFi) Applications**
- **NFT Marketplaces**
- **Decentralized Applications (dApps)**
- **Commit Log Checkers/Monitors**
- **Event Emit Filtering**
- **Optimizing Gas Fees** (e.g., gas-free ERC-20 transfers using signature generation)
- **Smart Contract Indexing**
- **Access Control Management**
- **OpenZeppelin Reentrancy Guards**
- **Chainlink Price Feeds**
- And much more...

Our focus will be on **security**, **best practices**, and **optimizing smart contract functionality** to build robust and efficient blockchain applications.
